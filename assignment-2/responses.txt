Briefly describe one way how data travels on the internet.
Data is separated into packets and then they travel through routers to a DNS
server that will reorder the packets back into the proper file.

What is the relationship between IP address and the DNS?
The DNS connects your IP address to your intended IP address of the website you
are visiting.

What is a cookie?
Data like saved passwords, names, addresses that are stored on a website from a
user's computer.

List the the three basic parts of a URL.
Protocol, Domain Name, Path. In that order.
